Privacy Policy

Kurshok Space does not track users, it has no analytics or statistics system. There is no data collection for things like page clicks, post views, or things like that. It is only with votes and subscriptions as a matter of functionality, that any data is stored, and all that this data is, is the user ID and what they voted on or subscribed to, as well as when they did so. Aside from emails (when provided for the purpose of authentication and notification messages), there are no personally identifying data collected by Kurshok Space, what is stored are essentially: emails, usernames, session cookies for the authentication system, ip addresses (for ratelimiting & anti-spam), and the contents and metadata (title, timestamp, tags, user IDs) associated with posts, messages, and comments.

Thereby, a user should be aware that by using Kurshok Space they are voluntarily consenting to the use of session cookies to facilitate the website's authentication functionalities and to prevent spam and misuse (ratelimiting). These cookies are not used for tracking, only for security purposes. Users may delete their account and its associated data at any time, or ask that an admin do so for them if there are technical problems or if things are still underconstruction.

Kurshok Space is completely passwordless, it authenticates users via authentication links otherwise known magic links send by email.

User generated content, like posts and comments, on Kurshok Space may be deleted by authors or by an administrator at their own discretion if and only if the content is terribly disagreeable. In the event that some technical fault or lack of sophistication in the software prevents a user from removing their content, they can contact an admin and ask that it be removed. That said, Kurshok Space is at the mercy of what users choose to comment or post, wherefore if there are issues with certain content, for example being unlawful or infringing on copyright or some such issue, upon becoming aware of this Kurshok Space may without warning remove the offending content and potentially delete the user account responsible for it. Kurshok Space is not responsible for any personal information that a user may disclose in their own posts, profiles, and comments.

If you as a user of Kurshok Space have issues with the website in light of any of the above, then feel free to contact a Kurshok Space admin via this email address: admin@Kurshok.space
